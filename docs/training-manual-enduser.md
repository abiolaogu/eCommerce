## 1. Introduction

### 1.1 Training Objectives
Upon completing this training, end users will be able to:
- Navigate the FusionCommerce storefront confidently
- Search for and browse products using filters and categories
- Add items to the shopping cart and complete checkout
- Participate in group buying campaigns
- Track orders and shipments
- Manage account settings and preferences

### 1.2 Training Duration
Estimated total training time: 4 hours across 3 modules.

### 1.3 Prerequisites
- A web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- A valid email address for account registration
- Internet connectivity

## 2. Module 1: Getting Started with FusionCommerce (1 hour)

### 2.1 Learning Objectives
- Create and configure your FusionCommerce account
- Navigate the storefront layout
- Understand key features and where to find them

### 2.2 Creating Your Account

**Step 1**: Open your web browser and navigate to the FusionCommerce storefront URL.

**Step 2**: Click the "Sign Up" button located in the top right corner of the page.

**Step 3**: Fill in the registration form:
- Full Name
- Email Address
- Password (minimum 8 characters, must include uppercase, lowercase, and a number)
- Confirm Password

**Step 4**: Click "Create Account" to submit.

**Step 5**: Check your email inbox for the verification message. Click the verification link to activate your account.

**Step 6**: Log in with your email and password.

### 2.3 Navigating the Storefront

The FusionCommerce storefront has these main sections:

| Section | Location | Purpose |
|---------|----------|---------|
| Navigation Bar | Top of page | Access categories, search, cart, and account |
| Search Bar | Top center | Find products by name, description, or SKU |
| Category Menu | Left sidebar or top menu | Browse products by category |
| Product Grid | Main content area | Display product listings |
| Cart Icon | Top right | Access shopping cart |
| Account Menu | Top right | Access profile, orders, and settings |
| Footer | Bottom of page | Help links, policies, and contact information |

### 2.4 Exercise: Explore the Storefront
1. Log in to your account
2. Click on 3 different product categories
3. Use the search bar to find a specific product
4. Click on a product to view its detail page
5. Locate the "My Account" section

### 2.5 Knowledge Check
- Q1: Where is the search bar located?
- Q2: How do you access your account settings?
- Q3: Where do you find your shopping cart?

## 3. Module 2: Shopping and Checkout (1.5 hours)

### 3.1 Learning Objectives
- Search and filter products effectively
- Add products to the cart and manage quantities
- Complete the checkout process
- Understand payment options

### 3.2 Searching for Products

**Basic Search**: Type a product name or keyword in the search bar and press Enter.

**Filtered Search**: After a search, use the sidebar filters to narrow results:
- **Price Range**: Set minimum and maximum price
- **Category**: Select a specific product category
- **Vendor**: Filter by marketplace seller
- **Availability**: Show only in-stock items
- **Rating**: Filter by customer rating threshold

**Sorting Options**:
- Relevance (default)
- Price: Low to High
- Price: High to Low
- Newest First
- Best Rated

### 3.3 Exercise: Find and Compare Products
1. Search for "headphones"
2. Apply a price filter ($50 - $200)
3. Sort by "Best Rated"
4. Click on the top 2 results to read product details
5. Use the "Compare" feature to compare them side-by-side

### 3.4 Adding Products to Cart

**Step 1**: On the product detail page, select any variant options (size, color, etc.).

**Step 2**: Set the desired quantity using the +/- buttons.

**Step 3**: Click "Add to Cart".

**Step 4**: A notification confirms the item has been added. You can:
- Continue shopping
- Go directly to the cart

### 3.5 Managing Your Cart

Access your cart by clicking the cart icon in the navigation bar.

**Update Quantity**: Use the +/- buttons next to each item.

**Remove Item**: Click the trash icon to remove an item.

**Apply Coupon**: Enter a promotion code in the "Coupon Code" field and click "Apply".

**Cart Summary** shows:
- Subtotal (items only)
- Estimated shipping
- Tax estimate
- Discount (if coupon applied)
- Total

### 3.6 Completing Checkout

**Step 1: Shipping Address**
- Select a saved address or enter a new one
- Choose a shipping method:
  - Standard (5-7 days)
  - Express (2-3 days)
  - Overnight (1 day)
  - Same-Day (where available)

**Step 2: Payment**
- Select a payment method:
  - Credit/Debit Card
  - Digital Wallet
  - Buy Now Pay Later (BNPL)
- Enter or confirm payment details

**Step 3: Review**
- Review all items, quantities, and prices
- Confirm shipping address and method
- Confirm payment method
- Review the final total

**Step 4: Place Order**
- Click "Place Order" to confirm
- Wait for the confirmation screen
- Note your order ID for reference
- Check your email for the order confirmation

### 3.7 Exercise: Complete a Purchase
1. Add 2 different products to your cart
2. Update the quantity of one item to 3
3. Apply a test coupon code (if provided by trainer)
4. Proceed to checkout
5. Enter a shipping address
6. Select Express Shipping
7. Enter payment information
8. Review and place the order
9. Note the order ID from the confirmation screen

### 3.8 Knowledge Check
- Q1: How do you filter products by price range?
- Q2: Where do you enter a coupon code?
- Q3: What are the four steps of the checkout process?

## 4. Module 3: Orders, Tracking, and Group Buying (1.5 hours)

### 4.1 Learning Objectives
- Track order status and shipments
- Understand the order lifecycle
- Participate in group buying campaigns
- Manage returns and refunds

### 4.2 Tracking Your Orders

**Step 1**: Click "My Account" in the navigation bar.

**Step 2**: Select "My Orders" from the menu.

**Step 3**: You will see a list of all your orders with their current status.

**Order Status Meanings**:

| Status Icon | Status | What It Means |
|-------------|--------|---------------|
| Clock | Created | Order received, being processed |
| Checkmark | Confirmed | Inventory reserved for your order |
| Dollar | Paid | Payment processed successfully |
| Truck | Shipped | Package dispatched with carrier |
| Home | Delivered | Package delivered to your address |
| X | Cancelled | Order was cancelled |

**Step 4**: Click on any order to see full details including:
- Item list with prices
- Status timeline showing each stage with timestamps
- Tracking information (when available)

### 4.3 Shipment Tracking

Once your order status changes to "Shipped":
1. Open the order detail page
2. Click "Track Shipment"
3. View the tracking timeline with:
   - Carrier name and tracking number
   - Current package location
   - Estimated delivery date
   - Historical location updates

You will also receive email and/or push notifications at each milestone.

### 4.4 Group Buying Campaigns

Group buying lets you get discounts by purchasing together with other shoppers.

**How It Works**:
1. A campaign is created for a product with a group discount
2. A minimum number of participants is required
3. You join the campaign and invite others
4. If the minimum is reached before expiration, everyone gets the discount
5. If not enough people join, no one is charged

**Joining a Campaign**:
1. Browse the "Group Deals" section on the storefront
2. View campaign details: product, discount, participants needed, time remaining
3. Click "Join This Campaign"
4. Confirm your participation
5. Share the campaign link with friends to help reach the goal

**Campaign Progress**:
- A progress bar shows current vs. required participants
- A countdown timer shows time remaining
- You receive notifications when new participants join
- You receive a final notification when the campaign succeeds or expires

### 4.5 Exercise: Participate in Group Buying
1. Navigate to the "Group Deals" page
2. Find an active campaign
3. Review the campaign details (product, discount, participants needed)
4. Join the campaign
5. Copy the sharing link
6. Check the campaign progress after other participants join
7. Observe the outcome (success or expiration)

### 4.6 Returns and Refunds

**Initiating a Return**:
1. Go to "My Orders"
2. Click on the delivered order
3. Click "Return Items"
4. Select items to return
5. Choose a reason for the return
6. Select return method (drop-off or scheduled pickup)
7. Print the return shipping label
8. Ship the items back

**Refund Timeline**:
- Refund processed within 5-7 business days after return is received
- Refund credited to original payment method
- Email confirmation sent when refund is processed

## 5. Account Management

### 5.1 Profile Settings
- Update your name, email, and phone number
- Change your password
- Enable two-factor authentication for added security

### 5.2 Address Book
- Save multiple shipping addresses
- Set a default address for faster checkout
- Edit or delete saved addresses

### 5.3 Payment Methods
- Add or remove credit/debit cards
- View digital wallet balance
- Manage BNPL payment plans

### 5.4 Notification Preferences
Customize which notifications you receive:
- Order status updates
- Group buying campaign updates
- Promotional offers
- Price drop alerts for wishlist items

Choose delivery method for each: Email, Push Notification, or SMS.

## 6. Tips for a Great Shopping Experience

1. **Save your address and payment** -- Speeds up checkout for future orders
2. **Use filters** -- Narrow down large catalogs to find what you need quickly
3. **Watch group deals** -- Share campaigns with friends for bigger discounts
4. **Enable notifications** -- Stay updated on order status and price drops
5. **Check "Saved for Later"** -- Items you moved from cart remain accessible
6. **Read reviews** -- Other customers' experiences help you make informed decisions

## 7. Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Cannot log in | Check email/password, use "Forgot Password" if needed |
| Product shows "Out of Stock" | Check back later or enable price drop alert |
| Payment declined | Verify card details and available funds |
| Order not showing in "My Orders" | Refresh the page; allow a few minutes for processing |
| Tracking not updating | Allow 24 hours after shipping for initial update |
| Coupon code not working | Check expiration date and minimum order requirements |
